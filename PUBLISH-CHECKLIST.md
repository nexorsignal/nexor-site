# NEXOR SIGNAL — Publish Checklist & V1 Structure

## Homepage on day one
Keep the homepage tight and signal-first.

1. **Hero**
   - NEXOR SIGNAL mark
   - "Signal Over Noise"
   - Short positioning line
   - 1 main CTA to latest signal note

2. **Signal Notes**
   - Show only the latest 3 notes
   - These are the short write-ups adapted from TikTok videos/tests

3. **Featured Essay**
   - Keep one featured long-form essay only

4. **Live Market Snapshot**
   - Small, clean, useful
   - AI / compute / crypto / semis focus
   - Do not let this become a giant dashboard

5. **Support**
   - Keep only 2 support actions on the homepage:
     - Support the Signal
     - Buy Me a Coffee

6. **Newsletter**
   - Single compact subscribe block near the bottom

## What to keep out of the homepage
To stay clean, do **not** overfill the homepage with:
- too many cards
- too many support options
- long About copy
- dense market tables
- repeated archive links
- extra creator fluff

## NEXOR SIGNAL v1 site structure

### Primary navigation
- Home
- Signal Notes
- Essays
- Markets
- Archive
- About

### Core content roles
- **Signal Notes** = 2–3 minute readable posts adapted from TikTok videos/tests
- **Essays** = longer thinking pieces
- **Markets** = clean live snapshot + related market notes
- **Archive** = everything in one place
- **About** = simple positioning, not a long manifesto
- **Support** = Buy Me a Coffee + Phoenix Wallet / Bitcoin support

## Launch notes
- Publish with at least **3 Signal Notes** and **1 Featured Essay**
- Ensure Buy Me a Coffee link is live
- Ensure Phoenix Wallet QR and invoice are current
- Check market API in production environment after deploy
- Test mobile nav, support buttons, and newsletter fallback

## Minimalism rule
If something does not strengthen the signal, remove it.
